What is Foundation 13?

We bring together recent school leavers and alumni to share real stories, offer practical guidance, open doors to new opportunities, and strengthen wellbeing through community.

Foundation 13 exists for the years after school — when life gets real and the path isn't always clear. Here's how we help young people grow together.

Who is Foundation 13 for?

Foundation 13 supports:

  • Year 12 and recent school leavers
  • Alumni in their first 10 years out of school
  • Older alumni who want to mentor and give back
  • Staff and coaches who support young people in transition

About Foundation 13

Foundation 13 was created to support the transition after school — the “Year 13” experience. Our mission is to help young people stay connected, find direction, and grow through community.

What we believe

  • Alumni have powerful wisdom to share.
  • No young person should face transition alone.
  • Connections create opportunity.
  • Sharing real stories builds confidence.
  • Community strengthens wellbeing.
